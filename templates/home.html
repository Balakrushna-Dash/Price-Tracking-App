About ::

This is a non-profitable free website made to track products on certain e-commerce websites. This tracker is only meant for Flipkart website products. (Although, In the future, we may expand it to other e-commerce websites ) If for any reason we are not able to track your product, we will right away inform you about our inability. In the "Add" tab, Give the correct URL of the product you wish to track. By saying "correct" we mean, choose the product ,click on it, copy the url of the page (the page on which "Buy now/Add to kart" is given) and paste it . if the price goes below the threshold price defined by you , we will send you a notification (by registered email) and stop tracking that product. We provide you a facility to track atmost {{numMax}} products at a time. If you have already exhausted them all, then you have to cancel some of those before adding another URL. The process of cancellation is very simple -> go to "My Url list"and cancel.
